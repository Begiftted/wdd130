Rivers State

Rivers State, located in Nigeria’s Niger Delta region, is known as the “Treasure Base of the Nation” for its rich oil and gas resources. Its capital, Port Harcourt, is a major industrial and commercial hub. The state is also blessed with diverse cultures, vibrant festivals, fertile lands, and an extensive network of rivers and creeks that shape its economy and lifestyle.
