Rivers State

Rivers State is one of Nigeria's 36 states, located in the Niger Delta region of the country's South-South geopolitical zone. The state, nicknamed "The Treasure Base of the Nation," is a major hub for the oil and gas industry, which drives its economy. Rivers State is known for its diverse geography, rich culture, and significant economic contributions.
