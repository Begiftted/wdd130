Detailed Trip Adventures

The Beginner's Glide

Perfect for families and first-timers! This half-day trip covers smooth, scenic waters with gentle currents and breathtaking views of the surrounding canyons. A great way to build confidence on the water.

  • **Duration:** Half Day (3 hours)
  • **Difficulty:** Class I-II
  • **Minimum Age:** 6 years

The Canyon Explorer

A full-day intermediate adventure for those seeking a bit more excitement. You'll tackle consistent Class III rapids and enjoy a riverside lunch. Requires basic fitness and a willingness to paddle hard!

  • **Duration:** Full Day (6 hours)
  • **Difficulty:** Class III
  • **Minimum Age:** 12 years

The Dragon's Fury

An overnight, advanced trip for experienced thrill-seekers. This journey features challenging Class IV and V whitewater, requiring strong teamwork and paddling skills. Includes camping and two full days on the river.

  • **Duration:** Two Days
  • **Difficulty:** Class IV-V
  • **Minimum Age:** 18 years
